# The Moonlit Library

## Level 1: The Locked Door

The player arrives at a quiet library under a silver moon. A brass door blocks the way into the story vault. Three symbols glow on the door: a candle, a key, and a book.

Challenge: Ask the player which symbol opens the story vault.

Answer: book.

Hint: The vault protects stories, and stories live inside this object.

## Level 2: The Whispering Shelf

Inside the vault, a shelf whispers that only a careful listener can continue. It says, "I have pages but no voice, yet I can speak inside your mind."

Challenge: Ask the player what the whispering shelf is describing.

Answer: book.

Hint: It cannot make sound by itself, but it can still tell a tale.

## Level 3: The Starry Bridge

A bridge of stars appears between two balconies. The bridge brightens only when the player names the light that guided them to the library.

Challenge: Ask the player what light guided them here.

Answer: moon.

Hint: It was shining in the sky when the adventure began.

## Ending

When the player completes every level, the story vault opens and releases a glowing tale. Congratulate the player, tell them they have completed the Moonlit Library, and invite them to return for another adventure.
